Ajitama

Description

This is the recipe for a basic chashu, compatible with any ramen that comes to mind

Ingredients

  • 1 kg pork belly
  • 5 dehydrated shiitake mushrooms
  • Water
  • Shoyu
  • Mirin
  • Kombu

Steps

  1. Add the chashu to a pan, fill with 1/2 water 1/2 shoyu
  2. Add the shiitake and mirin
  3. Cook lol
